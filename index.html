ABOUT
Playlist Plunderer 2 is an advanced macOS app for managing audio playlists and beep-testing!
Quickly create, edit, and beep-test curated sets of tracks—perfect for DJs, radio hosts, and more.
Features advanced search, batch tagging, timed beep modes, Apple Silicon & Intel builds, and more.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
TUTORIAL
Follow these steps to get started quickly:
1) Launch the app.
2) Import or create a playlist.
3) Enable “beep mode” if needed.
4) Play or export your curated set!
